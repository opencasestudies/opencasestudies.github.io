Meet our OCS team

Stephanie Hicks

Stephanie is an Assistant Professor at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health. She is a faculty member in the Johns Hopkins Data Science Lab and teaches Advanced Data Science. She is also creating a children’s book featuring women statisticians and data scientists.

Leah Jager

Leah is an Assistant Scientist in the Biostatistics Department at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health where she teaches biostatistics to undergraduate and masters students in Public Health. She has a strong interest in statistics education and is currently studying how people interact with data with the goal of being able to teach them more effectively.

Margaret Taub

Margaret is an Assistant Scientist in the Biostatistics Department at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Public Health.
